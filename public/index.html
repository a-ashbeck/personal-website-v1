A little about myself

Hi, my name is Alan, and I am a Software Engineer living in Chicago, IL. Currently, I work at Northwestern University's Center for Behavioral Intervention Technologies (CBITs). I primarily work with Ruby (on Rails), JavaScript, NodeJS, Express, Handlebars, React, Cordova, Bootstrap, MySQL, and PostgreSQL.

I spend the majority of my free time building things. Such things include guitars, metalsmithed trinkets, wooden objects, small (usually music related) electronics, and anything else I can take apart and put together. I enjoy playing guitar, cooking, fine whiskies, and secret powerlifting.
